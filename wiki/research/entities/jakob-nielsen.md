## Summary

Jakob Nielsen, Ph.D., is a Danish-American user-usability consultant and one of the world's leading figures in User Experience (UX). He co-founded the **Nielsen Norman Group** (NN/g) with Don Norman in 1998. He is famous for establishing cheap usability engineering methods (like Heuristic Evaluation) and defining the **10 Usability Heuristics for User Interface Design**.

## Key Contributions

- **10 Usability Heuristics** (1994) — A set of broad rules of thumb for interface design that have remained a gold standard for over three decades.
- **Heuristic Evaluation** — A usability engineering method for finding usability problems in a user interface design, making it part of an iterative design process.
- **Nielsen's Law of Internet Bandwidth** — A law stating that a high-end user's connection speed grows by 50% per year (slower than Moore's Law for processor power).
- **Sun Microsystems** — Served as a Distinguished Engineer at Sun Microsystems, focusing on making enterprise software and web interfaces easier to use.
