## Summary

Sarah Gibbons is a design strategist, user experience (UX) expert, and the Vice President at **Nielsen Norman Group** (NN/g). She specializes in design strategy, mapped representations (e.g., empathy mapping, customer journey mapping, service blueprinting), and Design Operations (DesignOps). She is the author of NN/g's foundational articles on the **Design Thinking** process.

## Key Contributions

- **Design Thinking Framework Mapping** — Authored "Design Thinking 101," consolidating and formalizing the 6-phase iterative model (Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, Test, Implement) under the three meta-categories: *Understand, Explore, Materialize*.
- **Empathy Mapping Guidelines** — Standardized modern user-experience practices around empathy mapping (defining what users say, think, do, and feel).
- **DesignOps Advocacy** — Promotes the optimization of design processes, tools, and teams to scale design impact in large organizations.
