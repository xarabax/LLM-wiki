## Summary

IBM (International Business Machines Corporation), nicknamed "Big Blue," is an American multinational technology corporation headquartered in Armonk, New York. IBM is a pioneer in computer hardware, software, cloud infrastructure, and cognitive computing. In the AI domain, IBM is famous for **Watson** (which won *Jeopardy!* in 2011) and for establishing early frameworks for enterprise AI design and ethics.

## Key AI Initiatives & Frameworks

- **IBM Watson** — A suite of enterprise AI services, applications, and APIs (such as Watson Assistant for chatbots and Watson Discovery for data mining).
- **IBM Principles for the AI Era** — IBM's corporate principles asserting that:
  1. The purpose of AI is to augment human intelligence (not replace it).
  2. Data and insights belong to their creator.
  3. Technology must be transparent, explainable, and free from bias.
- **IBM Design for AI** — A comprehensive design practice guiding creators to build trust, ethics, and conversational capability into AI interfaces.
